Mobile Setup

If you are a mobile user, then you have options such as Frosty and Chatsen. If you are on iOS, which both are also available on Android. But if you are on Android, you also have the additional option of DankChat.

Additional Content

Once you have installed Frosty all you have do is sign in and you are ready to go. The BTTV, FFZ, and 7TV emotes will be in the emote menu which will appear on the right side of the chatbox when you are in a channel.

Frosty

Chatsen is similar to Chatty or Chatterino. It uses a tab system and you can switch between chats and channels on the fly but you still have the ability to watch your favorite streamers. You can add chats by selecting the plus sign. The app explains most of the features during setup.

Chatsen

DankChat only provides you with the chat and the 3rd party emotes so this is only really designed for if you need a chat if you are streaming and can't pull it up on screen. However, it is still an option if all you need is a chat.
