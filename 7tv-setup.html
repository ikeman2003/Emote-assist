What's This?

There's no doubt you've been on Twitch at least once, and have seen random words like KEKW, LETHIMCOOK, or OMEGALUL and have likely been very confused and just shrugged off as nothing. Or maybe you even have the means necessary to see some of them, yet you still see them sometimes. But I have the solution for both problems.

If you are in case number one, then you need to get an emote extension. Which you can get links to, whichever extension of your choosing via the three links above. An emote extension is basically an extension that will convert these text into emotes via third party API. If you're just starting out, then there really isn't a wrong answer when you're choosing one of the three. So long as you have the correct options on, so you can see the emotes from the other two parties. And if you are in case #2, no matter which of the three you are using, you can see all three platforms and I'll show you how.
