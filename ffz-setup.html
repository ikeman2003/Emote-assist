Main Content

FFZ (FrankerFaceZ)

If you are here, then you have chosen the FFZ extension, which is short for FrankerFaceZ. FFZ is known for its high ability of customization as well as its native integration with third party tools, including the Twitch pronoun integration.

This will contain two methods of instruction, one of which via a video and the other will be purely text. You can follow however you wish or use them both if you wish.

Additional Content

This text will be assuming that you already have the FFZ extension installed.

To enable all emote functionality, you will have to go to the FFZ control center, which is located in the top right corner of your screen, and should look like the FFZ logo. See figure one.

Figure 1



Once you were in the FFZ control panel, locate and go to the add on section. Once you are there, you will need to enable the 7TV emotes and BTTV emotes add-ons. See figure 2.

Figure 2



Once you have completed that, you have successfully enabled the 7TV emotes and BTTV emotes add-ons.
