BTTV (BetterTTV)

If you are here, then you have chosen the BTTV extension, which is short for BetterTTV. BTTV was one of the first of its kind and still is updated today. However, there is no native integration with the Twitch pronoun system Which will require the additional extension provided by alejo.

This will contain two methods of instruction, one of which via a video and the other will be purely text. You can follow however you wish or use them both if you wish.

Additional Content

This text will be assuming that you already have the BTTV extension installed.

First, you must get to the BTTV settings, which is located under the profile picture, then under BetterTTV settings. See figure 1.

Figure 1

Once you have opened the BTTV settings, you will need to scroll down until you see 7TV emotes. You will need to check the box. Then a new one will appear below it. You will want to check that one too. When you receive a prompt when clicking the second check box, just click continue. See figure 2.

Figure 2

Once you have completed this, you have successfully enabled the necessary settings to see all three platforms emotes.
